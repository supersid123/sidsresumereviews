About Me

Welcome to Sid's Resume Reviews! I’m Siddarth Srivastava, a data science professional and career mentor with a passion for helping others navigate their career journeys. My experience spans across data analytics, strategic advising, and career guidance, enabling me to provide actionable insights for crafting impactful resumes.

My Journey

I graduated from UC Berkeley with a degree in Data Science and have worked with companies like Amazon and NBCUniversal. These experiences not only honed my technical skills but also deepened my understanding of how to stand out in competitive job markets.

My Experience

During my time at UC Berkeley, I held mentorship roles, advising students on resume building, career strategy, and navigating the job market. This experience helped me understand the challenges job seekers face and motivated me to start Sid's Resume Reviews to offer personalized, effective guidance.

Why I Do This

I believe that every individual has a unique story, and my goal is to help you tell yours through a compelling resume. I’m dedicated to providing detailed, personalized feedback to help you land interviews and succeed in your career.
